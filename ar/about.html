عن شركتنا

YudaHualong Concrete is a professional manufacturer specializing in concrete mixing equipment R&D and production. تقع في تشنغتشو، خنان، الصين, we have over 25 years of industry experience and are dedicated to providing comprehensive one-stop services to customers worldwide. Since establishment, we have consistently upheld the corporate spirit of "pursuing quality, creating value," continuously striving for excellence and innovation. Our superior products and services have earned trust and praise from customers globally.

As one of China's large-scale construction machinery manufacturers, لدينا قاعدة إنتاج بمساحة 40,000 متر مربع featuring advanced technology, equipment, and processes. نحن معتمدون وفقاً لـ ISO9001, ensuring every machine meets the highest industry standards before delivery. Our registered capital was increased to 12 million RMB in 2025, reflecting continuous growth and strong financial standing.
