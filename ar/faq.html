What countries do you export to?+

How long does production and delivery take?+
Standard production takes 15-30 days depending on the model. Shipping to Central Asia typically takes 12-18 days by rail.
Do you offer after-sales support and spare parts?+
Yes. Lifetime spare parts supply and remote technical support are included. Available 24/7 via phone and email.
What's the difference between mobile and stationary plants?+
Mobile plants require no foundation and can be relocated easily —?ideal for road projects and short-term sites. Stationary plants offer higher capacity and are best for permanent commercial concrete production.
