JS Twin-Shaft Malaxeur à Béton

Complete JS series twin-shaft forced malaxeur à bétons for batching plants and independent operations

JS series twin-shaft forced malaxeur à béton

Product Description

JS series twin-shaft forced malaxeur à béton features independent operation capability and the dual advantage of forming a simple centrale à béton when combined with PLD series batching machines. It can also serve as the main mixer for complete batching plants, enabling automated operation and intelligent management. Suitable for various industrial and civil construction projects including roads, bridges, water conservancy, docks, and buildings.

Performance Caractéristiques

High Efficiency & Stability

Twin-shaft forced mixing principle ensures fast and uniform concrete mixing. High-efficiency copper-wound mixing motor improves production efficiency.

Easy Maintenance

Mixing drum and blades are made of high-strength wear-resistant materials, extending service life and reducing maintenance costs while ensuring concrete quality and stability.

Advanced Technology

Advanced electronic and sensor technology enables intelligent control and precise monitoring of equipment. Real-time monitoring and adjustment of operating parameters optimizes production process.

Environmental Protection

Low noise and low energy consumption equipment and processes reduce environmental impact.
