HZS Series Overview

YUDA provides complete HZS concrete batching plant solutions from 25m³/h to 480m³/h for different construction requirements. Each plant features our JS twin-shaft forced mixer for superior concrete quality, PLC automatic control for reliable operation, and precise electronic weighing for accurate batching. Whether you need a compact plant for a small project or a high-capacity plant for commercial supply, the HZS series delivers professional-grade performance.
