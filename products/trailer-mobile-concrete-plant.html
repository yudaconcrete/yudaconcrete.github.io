Trailer-Mounted Mobile Concrete Plant

The trailer mobile concrete batching plant is our most portable solution. Mounted on a heavy-duty trailer chassis, it can be towed by a standard truck from one project site to another without any disassembly. It arrives ready to produce concrete within hours of arrival.

No Foundation Required

No concrete base needed. Level the ground and start producing.

Quick Relocation

Tow by truck to next site. No disassembly required.

Fast Setup

Operational within hours, not days or weeks.

Multi-Project Use

Move between sites. One machine serves multiple projects.

Typical Applications

  • Road and highway construction
  • Bridge and tunnel projects
  • Remote mining and energy projects
  • Emergency construction after natural disasters
  • Small to medium size infrastructure projects
