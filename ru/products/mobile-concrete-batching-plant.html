Mobile concrete batching plant

Product Description

The mobile concrete batching plant consists of mixing system, weighing system, conveying system, storage system, control system and other auxiliary facilities. It features high mechanization and automation, high production efficiency, and long-term operational stability. Equipped with wheels and connected to power, it can start production immediately, significantly reducing transportation time and cost while keeping concrete fresh supply.
