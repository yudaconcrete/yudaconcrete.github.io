Drum type trailer mobile concrete plant for flexible construction operations

Trailer mobile concrete plant

Product Description

The trailer mobile concrete plant consists of mixing system, weighing system, conveying system, storage system, control system and other auxiliary facilities. With wheels mounted and power connected, it can start production immediately, significantly reducing transportation time and cost while keeping concrete fresh supply. The drum mixing system is suitable for small to medium projects requiring frequent relocation.
