FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

¿Cuánto cuesta una planta de hormigón?+
Price depends on capacity, configuration, and automation level. Contact us with your project requirements for a detailed factory-direct quotation.
Do you provide installation service?+
Yes. Ofrecemos orientación completa de instalación y soporte de puesta en marcha in situ. Nuestro equipo técnico puede viajar a su proyecto for smooth setup and operation.
What countries do you export to?+
We export to Russia, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Nigeria, Pakistan, Kenya, Saudi Arabia, Vietnam, and more. We handle international logistics, customs clearance, and documentation.
What is the delivery time?+
Standard production takes 15-30 days depending on the model. Shipping to Central Asia typically takes 12-18 days by rail. Sea freight to Africa and Middle East available.
Do you offer after-sales support?+
Yes. Lifetime spare parts supply and 24/7 remote technical support are included. Available by phone and email for urgent inquiries.
