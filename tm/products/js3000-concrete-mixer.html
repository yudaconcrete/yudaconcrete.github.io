Бетоносмеситель JS3000

High-performance forced mixer for concrete batching plants and independent operations

JS3000 twin-shaft forced concrete mixer

Описание продукта

JS series twin-shaft forced concrete mixer features independent operation capability and the dual advantage of forming a simple concrete batching plant when combined with PLD series batching machines. It can also serve as the main mixer for complete batching plants, enabling automated operation and intelligent management. Suitable for various industrial and civil construction projects including roads, bridges, water conservancy, docks, and buildings.

Performance Особенности

High Efficiency & Stability

Twin-shaft forced mixing principle ensures fast and uniform concrete mixing. High-efficiency copper-wound mixing motor improves production efficiency.

Mixing drum and blades are made of high-strength wear-resistant materials, extending service life and reducing maintenance costs while ensuring concrete quality and stability.

Intelligent Control

Advanced electronic and sensor technology enables intelligent control and precise monitoring of equipment. Real-time monitoring and adjustment of operating parameters optimizes production process.

Low noise and low energy consumption equipment and processes reduce environmental impact.
