HZS series usina de concreto

Series Overview

The HZS series usina de concreto is a forced-type, high-efficiency equipment capable of producing various types of concrete including plastic concrete and dry-hard concrete. With high production efficiency, it is widely used in large and medium-scale building construction, road and bridge engineering, as well as precast concrete product manufacturing. It is the ideal equipment for commercial concrete production.

Key Features

JS Misturador Forçado de Duplo Eixo

JS series twin-shaft forced mixer delivers uniform concrete quality, shorter mixing cycles, durable wear-resistant components, and easy maintenance access for long-term operation.

Electronic Weighing System

Electronic weighing with microcomputer control and digital display. Buffer device and automatic compensation ensure high measurement accuracy.

PLC Automatic Control

Advanced PLC control system enables fully automatic operation, fault self-diagnosis, production data recording, and one-button start. Energy consumption reduced by 15%.

Wide Belt Conveyor Feeding

Sand and aggregate feeding system with wide chevron belt conveyor and maintenance walkway ensures stable material supply.
