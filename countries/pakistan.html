Construction Market in Pakistan

Pakistan's construction industry is growing rapidly, driven by the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC) infrastructure projects, housing schemes, and commercial development in major cities like Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad. Road construction, dam projects, and real estate development create sustained demand for concrete batching equipment.
