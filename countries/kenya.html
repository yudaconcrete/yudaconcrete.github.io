Concrete Plants for Kenya

Durable concrete batching solutions for Kenya's construction sector. Mobile plants for road infrastructure projects and stationary plants for commercial concrete supply in Nairobi and Mombasa.

Construction Market in Kenya

Kenya is East Africa's largest economy with ambitious infrastructure development under the Big Four Agenda and Vision 2030. The Nairobi Expressway, Standard Gauge Railway extensions, road network expansion, and rapid urbanization in Nairobi, Mombasa, and Kisumu create sustained demand for concrete batching equipment. The growing real estate sector also drives need for reliable concrete supply.
