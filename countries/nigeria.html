Construction Market in Nigeria

Nigeria is Africa's largest economy with massive infrastructure needs. The government's infrastructure investment plan, rapid urbanization in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, and the growing real estate sector drive strong demand for concrete equipment. Road construction, housing development, and commercial projects require reliable concrete production solutions.
