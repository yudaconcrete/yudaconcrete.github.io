5 Questions to Ask Before Buying a Concrete Plant

Buying a concrete batching plant is a major investment. Before you sign, ask these 5 questions: 1) What is my actual daily capacity need? 2) Is the supplier CE certified? 3) What support do they offer after sale? 4) How long for delivery and installation? 5) What do existing customers say?
